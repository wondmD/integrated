About This Application

The Wind Load Calculator is designed to assist engineers and architects in determining wind loads on building structures. It provides accurate calculations for wind pressures and forces, adhering to the Eurocode standard ES EN 1991-1-4:2015. The application supports two main categories:

  • Wind Load on Walls: Calculate wind pressures for vertical building surfaces.
  • Wind Load on Roofs: Analyze wind loads for various roof types, including Duopitch, Monopitch, Hipped, and Flat roofs.

Each calculation provides detailed step-by-step solutions, including formulas, input values, and references to the Eurocode standard, ensuring transparency and reliability.
